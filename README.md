Privacy Policy for BurnCopy: Secure Clipboard
Last Updated: March 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how BurnCopy ("we," "our," or "the App") handles your information. By using the App, you agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Information Collection and Use
BurnCopy is designed as a privacy-first utility.

Clipboard Data: The App accesses your device's clipboard to provide auto-deletion and "burning" services. This data is processed locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or transmit your clipboard content to any external servers.

Secure Log: The App maintains a local history of "Burn" events (timestamps). This data remains on your device and can be cleared by the user at any time.

2. Permissions
To function correctly, the App requires the following permissions:

Clipboard Access: To monitor and clear text data based on user settings.

Haptic Feedback: To provide physical notification (vibration) upon data deletion.

Foreground Service: To ensure the "Auto-Burn" timer completes accurately when the app is minimized.

3. Third-Party Services (Advertising)
While the current version may be ad-free, we reserve the right to include advertising in future updates. We use Google AdMob as our primary ad provider.

AdMob: May collect device identifiers, location data (if permitted), and interaction data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. You can view their policy here: Google Privacy & Terms.

4. Data Storage and Security
Local Storage only: All user settings and logs are stored in the App's private directory using SharedPreferences.

No Internet Transmission: The core functionality of the App does not require internet access, ensuring your copied data is never leaked online.

5. Children’s Privacy
Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.
